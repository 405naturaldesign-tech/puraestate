## FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

**SAN JOSÉ, COSTA RICA – February 24, 2026** – PuraEstate, a pioneering fintech platform, today announced the official launch of its AI-powered mobile application designed to revolutionize how investors discover, evaluate, and purchase premium real estate properties in Costa Rica. The platform combines cutting-edge artificial intelligence with human expertise to provide unprecedented market insights and investment opportunities to investors worldwide.

### The Problem

The Costa Rica real estate market has historically been fragmented, information-opaque, and challenging for international investors to navigate. Language barriers, cultural complexities, regulatory uncertainties, and limited access to comprehensive property data have kept qualified investors on the sidelines. Traditional real estate brokers lack digital sophistication, and property searching remains mired in outdated methodologies.

### The Solution

PuraEstate's mobile-first platform leverages advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze thousands of property listings, market trends, investment metrics, and environmental factors in real-time. The application provides:

- **AI-Powered Property Matching**: Machine learning algorithms match investors' preferences, budgets, and risk profiles with ideal properties
- **Comprehensive Market Analytics**: Real-time data on property values, rental yields, appreciation trends, and market cycles
- **Investment Scoring System**: Proprietary algorithm assesses investment potential on ROI, market position, and appreciation forecasts
- **Regulatory Compliance Dashboard**: Automated guidance on Costa Rican property law, tax implications, and legal requirements
- **Expert Network Access**: Direct connections to vetted lawyers, accountants, agents, and property managers
- **Currency & Payment Integration**: Seamless handling of USD, CRC, and cryptocurrency transactions with favorable exchange rates
- **Virtual Tours & 3D Analytics**: AI-enhanced property visualizations with environmental and infrastructure analysis
