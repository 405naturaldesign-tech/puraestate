## PRODUCT OVERVIEW

### PuraEstate Platform

**What It Is:**
An AI-powered mobile-first platform for international real estate investing in Costa Rica, combining machine learning, market data, regulatory guidance, and expert network access.

**Available On:**
- iOS App Store
- Google Play Store (Android)
- Web browser (www.puraestate.com)

**Key Features:**

1. **AI Property Matching**
   - Machine learning algorithms analyze investor profile
   - Compares preferences to 8,400+ properties across 47 data points each
   - Returns top 20 personalized matches ranked by investment potential
   - Time to match: <5 minutes

2. **Investment Scoring System**
   - Proprietary algorithm scores each property 1-10
   - Evaluates: financial potential, market position, risk factors, tax implications
   - Provides transparent explanation for every score
   - Helps investors avoid bad deals and identify hidden gems

3. **Market Analytics**
   - Real-time neighborhood trends
   - 5-year and 10-year appreciation data
   - Rental yield projections
   - Market cycle analysis
   - Infrastructure development tracking
   - Updated hourly

4. **Regulatory Compliance Guidance**
   - Automated explanation of Costa Rican property law
   - Tax treaty information
   - Investment agreement status guidance
   - Capital gains implications
   - Foreign ownership rules
   - Currency regulations

5. **Expert Network**
   - 50+ pre-vetted Costa Rican property lawyers
   - 40+ experienced property managers
   - 200+ real estate specialists
   - 30+ tax and accounting professionals
   - Direct messaging through app
   - Transparent pricing

6. **3D Virtual Tours & AI Analysis**
   - Explore properties remotely
   - AI environmental analysis
   - Infrastructure proximity assessment
   - Neighborhood walkability scores
   - Development potential analysis

7. **Portfolio Tracking**
   - Monitor multiple properties
   - Expense tracking and management
   - Rental income tracking
   - Return calculations (IRR, ROI, equity appreciation)
   - Comparable market analysis
   - Tax documentation organization

8. **Smart Notifications**
   - New properties matching your profile
   - Price drops on saved properties
   - Market insights and alerts
   - Competitive alerts
   - Regulatory updates
