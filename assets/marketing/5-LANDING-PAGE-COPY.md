**Q4: Is my money safe?**

A: PuraEstate doesn't hold your money. We're a platform, not a bank.

When you make an offer:
1. You work with a Costa Rican lawyer (from our network)
2. The lawyer handles escrow (regulated by Costa Rican authorities)
3. You transfer funds directly to the lawyer's escrow account (not through PuraEstate)
4. At closing, funds are released

This is the standard Costa Rican process. Your money is protected by Costa Rican law and held by licensed lawyers.

## CONVERSION OPTIMIZATION

### Best Practices Implemented:

1. **Clear Value Prop Above the Fold**
   - Immediate benefit communication
   - Problem-solution framework
   - Specific numbers and guarantees

2. **Social Proof Early**
   - Testimonials visible on homepage
   - User numbers and ratings
   - Specific use cases and results

3. **Clear CTA**
   - Primary CTA (Download App)
   - Secondary CTA (Demo/Call)
   - Tertiary CTA (Pricing)
   - Every section has CTA

4. **Feature-Benefit Format**
   - Not just features, but benefits to user
   - Addresses specific pain points
   - Explains value clearly

5. **Addressing Objections**
   - FAQ section (15 common questions)
   - Transparent pricing
   - Trust signals (expert network, regulations)
   - Security explanation

6. **Urgency**
   - Founder's Club limited time offer
   - Limited to 5,000 members
   - "Don't miss out" messaging in appropriate places

7. **Mobile Optimization**
   - All copy works on mobile
   - CTAs are thumb-friendly
   - Load times are fast
   - Forms are short
