## EMAIL OPTIMIZATION NOTES

**A/B Testing Strategy:**
- Test subject lines for maximum open rate
- Test send times for optimal engagement
- Track click-through rates per CTA
- Monitor unsubscribe rates (should be <0.5%)

**Personalization Elements:**
- Use recipient's first name in salutation
- Segment by audience (investors vs. passive interest)
- Reference earlier engagement signals if available
- Include localized content (Spanish version for Hispanic subscribers)

**Performance Targets:**
- Email 1: 25-35% open rate (teaser)
- Email 2: 35-45% open rate (launch announcement)
- Email 3: 20-25% open rate (day 3 fatigue setting in)
- Email 4: 18-22% open rate (feature deep-dive)
- Email 5: 25-35% open rate (urgency/last chance)
- Overall click-through rate: 5-8% minimum
- Conversion rate (download): 8-12% minimum
- Conversion rate (Founder's Club): 3-5% of downloaders

**Follow-up Sequences:**
- Non-openers: Resend day 2 with different subject
- Non-clickers: Send "last chance" email on day 6
- Downloaders: Onboarding sequence with feature tutorials
- Paid members: Welcome sequence + exclusive content
