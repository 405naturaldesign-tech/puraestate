## SUPPORT & TROUBLESHOOTING

### Email Not Sending?
- Check email provider connection
- Verify sender domain/IP
- Check spam filters
- Test with small segment first

### Low Downloads?
- Check app store listing is live
- Verify download links work
- Increase paid advertising
- Reach out to influencers for emergency promotion
- Check email delivery rates

### Low Social Engagement?
- Post during peak hours (adjust times in timeline)
- Increase posting frequency
- Use video instead of images
- Engage with comments immediately
- Try viral formats (polls, questions, etc.)

### App Store Ranking Issues?
- Verify app is searchable
- Check keyword optimization
- Request feature placement
- Encourage reviews early
- Look for ranking barriers
