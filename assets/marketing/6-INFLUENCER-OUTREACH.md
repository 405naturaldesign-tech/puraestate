## MESSAGING GUIDELINES FOR INFLUENCERS

### Brand Voice:
- Expert but accessible
- Data-driven but human
- Honest about risks
- Enthusiastic about opportunity
- Not "get rich quick"

### Key Messages to Emphasize:
1. **Democratization:** Previously only accessible to institutions
2. **Information:** First unified platform for market intelligence
3. **Simplicity:** Complex market made accessible
4. **Returns:** Better yields, appreciation than US market
5. **Risk:** Real opportunity with real risks
6. **Timing:** First-mover advantage available now

### Messages to Avoid:
- "Get rich quick" language
- Guaranteed returns
- Speculation about future prices
- Dismissing risks
- Overhyped claims

### Authentic Framing:
- "I tried PuraEstate and here's what I found..."
- "For investors interested in Costa Rica..."
- "This doesn't replace doing your own due diligence, but..."
- "Here's what the platform does well..."
- "Here's where you need additional research..."
