## 1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the Platform function, improve performance, and provide a better user experience.

## 4. Your Choices

### Cookie Banner
On your first visit, you will see a cookie banner. You may:
- **Accept All:** Consent to all cookie categories
- **Customize:** Select which categories to allow
- **Reject Non-Essential:** Essential cookies only

### Withdrawing Consent
You may withdraw or change your consent at any time via:
- The cookie settings link in the website footer
- Your account preferences page
- Browser settings (block or delete cookies)

## 5. How to Manage Cookies

Most browsers allow you to control cookies:
- **Chrome:** Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
- **Firefox:** Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies
- **Safari:** Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
- **Edge:** Settings → Cookies and site permissions

Blocking essential cookies may prevent the Platform from functioning properly.
